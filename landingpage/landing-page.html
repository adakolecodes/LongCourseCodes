About Us

Otukpo Tech Academy is a non governmental organization that provides free IT training enabling the next generation to drive their own future in Nigeria. We are proudly supported by a UK Charity: The Back Home Project

Our Mission

Learn coding skills with us in Otukpo and you'll learn with the best: We provide high quality free IT training in small classes with fully functional IT equipment.

We offer:

  • Week long introduction to coding courses
  • 3 month intensive coding bootcamp
  • Small class sizes
  • All equipment provided
